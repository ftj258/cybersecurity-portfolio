# Chain of Custody Process

## Purpose

Ensure integrity, authenticity, and traceability of digital evidence from acquisition to storage and presentation.

---

## Documentation Requirements

- Evidence identifier
- Description of device or artifact
- Date and time of acquisition
- Collector name and signature
- Storage location
- Transfer records

---

## Evidence Handling Procedure

1. Assign unique evidence ID
2. Photograph device or artifact
3. Record serial numbers and identifiers
4. Acquire forensic image using write blocker
5. Generate hash values (MD5/SHA256)
6. Secure evidence in tamper-evident storage

---

## Integrity Validation

- Hash verification before and after transfer
- Secure evidence locker storage
- Access logs maintained

---

## Risk Mitigation

- No direct analysis on original evidence
- All work performed on verified forensic copies
- Strict access control enforcement

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Evidence integrity protection
- Legal defensibility awareness
- Documentation discipline
